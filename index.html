Research areas

Drug policy

Criminalization, harm reduction, epistemic power, lived experience, and what the evidence actually shows

How policy shapes drug use outcomes, who bears the costs, whose knowledges are privileged in decision making, and where reform is overdue.

Environmental policy

Pollution, climate change, and who pays the price

Environmental burdens don't fall equally. I'm interested in the interaction of environmental policy, population health, and the communities that absorb the cost of inaction.

Health care policy

Access, equity, reform, and the architecture of American care

Examining how health systems are built, who they serve well and who they overlook, where reforms are needed, and how to optimize to improve health outcomes at the population level.
